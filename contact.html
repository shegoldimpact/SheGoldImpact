Frequently Asked Questions

How can I verify that my donation was received?

After making a donation, please send us a message via WhatsApp or email with your name and donation details. We'll verify receipt and send you an acknowledgment within 24 hours.

Are donations tax-deductible?

We are working on obtaining official nonprofit status. Currently, donations are not tax-deductible, but we provide official receipts for all contributions that may be useful for your records.

Can I specify which project my donation supports?

Yes! When making a donation, you can specify if you'd like it to support a particular project (e.g., Help Orphan Project, Medical Outreach). Otherwise, it will be used where most needed.

How often do you conduct donation drives?

We conduct major donation drives quarterly, with our largest event in December. We also respond to urgent needs throughout the year as they arise.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Absolutely! We have opportunities for remote volunteers in areas like social media management, graphic design, content writing, and virtual tutoring.
