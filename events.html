Our Event History

A journey of impact through consistent community engagement and humanitarian outreach since 2015

September 20, 2025

Fourth Annual Orphanage Donation

Successful donation drive providing food items, shoes, clothes, detergents and essential supplies to orphanages. This marked our fourth consistent annual support since 2020 under the SheGoldImpact brand, with record community participation.

View Full Gallery
December 2023

Third Annual Donation Drive

Continued support for orphanages with expanded reach and increased community participation. Strengthened our partnership model with local communities and established new relationships with corporate sponsors.

View Photos
December 2022

Second Annual Donation Drive

Building on previous success with more orphanages included in the support program. Established consistent annual donation pattern and developed systematic approach to beneficiary needs assessment.

View Photos
December 2021

Help Orphan Prokjet Expansion

Second year of the ongoing orphanage support initiative with increased donor participation and structured approach to resource allocation and impact measurement.

April 2020

First Help Orphan Prokjet Launch

Inaugural donation drive and Easter convention at BASCO orphanage, establishing our ongoing commitment to orphanage support under Rhema Prayer City Inc with interfaith collaboration.

2019

Help Orphan Prokjet Established

Formal establishment of HOP under Rhema Prayer City Inc with clear mission to care, feed, clothe and provide educational support to orphans through structured community programs.

2017

Project 100 Support

Partnership with Kwame Sefa Kayi's Kokrokoo Charities Foundation to support Project 100 for neonatal care equipment in hospitals nationwide, expanding our impact beyond local communities.

2015

Akropong School for the Blind

Our first major philanthropic project supporting visually challenged students with essential supplies and educational materials, planting the seeds for our ongoing humanitarian work.
