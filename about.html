About SheGoldImpact

From Humble Beginnings to Global, Interfaith Impact

Our Authentic Story

Our Roots in Compassion

Our story begins in the heart of Amanokrom-Akpapim, Eastern Region of Ghana, where faith and compassion intersected within Rhema Prayer Fellowship. What started as simple acts of kindness in 2015 has blossomed into a global, interfaith movement of hope.

Our Growing Interfaith Fellowship Family

Amanokrom-Akpapim - Our foundational community
Koforidua - Expanded presence in Eastern Region
Accra - Capital city outreach
International - Members in Canada, USA, and beyond
Interfaith Unity - Christian-led with Muslim affiliate members, united in service

Explore Our Journey

Our Mission

Guided by compassion and driven by purpose, we are committed to creating sustainable impact through targeted initiatives.

Care

Providing compassionate support and creating safe spaces for vulnerable communities through consistent presence and emotional support.

Clothe

Ensuring dignity through proper clothing and essential personal items that restore confidence and self-worth.

Feed

Addressing hunger and malnutrition through sustainable food programs and nutritional support for families in need.

Educate

Creating access to quality education and skill development opportunities for lifelong empowerment and growth.

Include

Building inclusive communities where everyone has a place and purpose, regardless of background or circumstance.

Our Journey Through Time

2015

First Outreach - Akropong School for the Blind

Inspired by fellowship and prayer, our interfaith Amanokrom members contributed towards supporting visually challenged students with educational materials and essential supplies.

2017

Project 100 - Kokrokoo Charities Foundation

Supported Kwame Sefa Kayi's initiative to provide incubators for preterm babies in deprived hospitals nationwide, expanding our impact beyond local communities.

2019

Help Orphan Project (HOP) Launch

Formally established HOP under Rhema Prayer City Inc with clear mission: Care, Feed, Clothe, and Provide Educational Support to orphans through structured programs.

2020

First Orphanage Visit & Easter Convention

Leader accompanied by three women ministers visited BASCO orphanage, delivered donations and preached at Easter convention, establishing ongoing relationship.

2021-2024

Sustained Impact & Growth

Continued annual donation drives, expanding reach and impact each year with interfaith collaboration and growing community support.

2025

SheGoldImpact & 4th Donation Drive

Formalized initiatives under SheGoldImpact brand with expanded vision. Conducted 4th major donation on September 20, 2025 with increased community participation.

Future

SheGold Foundation Vision

Planning transition to SheGold Foundation with expanded programs, sustainable impact model, and broader humanitarian reach across West Africa.
