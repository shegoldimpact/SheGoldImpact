About SheGoldImpact

From Humble Beginnings to Global, Interfaith Impact

Our Authentic Story

Our Roots in Compassion
Our story begins in the heart of Amanokrom-Akpapim, Eastern Region of Ghana, where faith and compassion intersected within Rhema Prayer Fellowship. What started as simple acts of kindness in 2015 has blossomed into a global, interfaith movement of hope.

Our Growing Interfaith Fellowship Family
• Amanokrom-Akpapim - Our foundational community
• Koforidua - Expanded presence in Eastern Region
• Accra - Capital city outreach
• International - Members in Canada, USA, and beyond
• Interfaith Unity - Christian-led with Muslim affiliate members, united in service

Our Mission

Guided by compassion and driven by purpose, we are committed to:

Care

Providing compassionate support and creating safe spaces for vulnerable communities

Clothe

Ensuring dignity through proper clothing and essential personal items

Feed

Addressing hunger and malnutrition through sustainable food programs

Educate

Creating access to quality education and skill development opportunities

Include

Building inclusive communities where everyone has a place and purpose

Our Journey

2015

First Outreach - Akropong School for the Blind

Inspired by fellowship and prayer, our interfaith Amanokrom members contributed towards supporting visually challenged students

2017

Project 100 - Kokrokoo Charities Foundation

Supported Kwame Sefa Kayi's initiative to provide incubators for preterm babies in deprived hospitals nationwide

2019

Help Orphan Project (HOP) Launch

Formally established HOP under Rhema Prayer City Inc with clear mission: Care, Feed, Clothe, and Provide Educational Support to orphans

2020

First Orphanage Visit & Easter Convention

Leader accompanied by three women ministers visited BASCO orphanage, delivered donations and preached at Easter convention

2021-2024

Sustained Impact

Continued annual donation drives, expanding reach and impact each year with interfaith collaboration

2025

SheGoldImpact & 4th Donation Drive

Formalized initiatives under SheGoldImpact brand with expanded vision. Conducted 4th major donation on September 20, 2025

Future

SheGold Foundation

Planning transition to SheGold Foundation with expanded programs and sustainable impact model
